About Gatara AI

Gatara AI’s mission is to simplify AI for everyone, making it accessible, intuitive, and transformative. About the role:

Are you a creative, innovative, and detail-oriented content creator with a passion for social media? If you answered "YES!" to that question, we have an amazing opportunity for you at Gatara AI!

As a Content Creator at Gatara AI, you’ll play a vital role in crafting engaging and impactful content for our Instagram and Facebook platforms. Your mission is to create high-quality, visually appealing content that not only promotes our brand but also drives engagement and interest in our AI SaaS products. You’ll bring fresh ideas to the table, tell stories that resonate, and help us communicate the transformative power of AI in a way that’s accessible and inspiring.
 

What You’ll Do:

  • Create engaging and visually stunning content for Instagram and Facebook, including posts, stories, reels, and ads.
  • Develop marketing content specifically for our AI SaaS product pages, showcasing their features, benefits, and use cases in a compelling way.
  • Collaborate with the team to brainstorm and execute creative content ideas that align with our brand and product goals.
  • Manage content calendars and ensure deadlines are met with high-quality deliverables.
  • Use digital tools and platforms to design, edit, and optimize content for maximum reach and engagement.
  • Stay up-to-date with trends in AI, technology, and social media to keep our content fresh and relevant.

What We’re Looking For:

  • A knack for storytelling and creating captivating content that resonates with diverse audiences.
  • Strong visual communication skills, with an eye for design and aesthetics.
  • Proficiency in using digital tools for content creation, editing, and design (e.g., Canva, Adobe Creative Suite, etc.).
  • Experience creating content for Instagram and Facebook, including familiarity with best practices for each platform.
  • A creative mindset with a problem-solving approach to content challenges.
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities.
  • Strong English skills to craft clear, engaging, and error-free captions and copy.

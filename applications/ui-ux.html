About Gatara AI

Gatara AI’s mission is to simplify AI for everyone, making it accessible, intuitive, and transformative. Our team is a rapidly growing group of passionate software developers, designers, and business leaders working together to build AI solutions that are not only powerful but also practical and inclusive.

About the role:

At Gatara AI, we believe that great design is not just about aesthetics—it’s about creating experiences that are simple, clear, catchy, and useful. We’re looking for a talented UI/UX Designer who shares this philosophy and can apply the "Don’t Make Me Think" strategy to design intuitive, user-friendly interfaces that drive engagement and satisfaction.

Your mission is to design interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also incredibly easy to use, ensuring that users can navigate our AI-powered SaaS products effortlessly. By creating designs that prioritize user experience, you’ll play a key role in increasing our profits, expanding our reach, and helping us achieve our mission of making AI accessible to everyone.
 

What You’ll Do:

  • Design user-friendly interfaces that are simple, clear, and intuitive, applying the "Don’t Make Me Think" philosophy to every project.
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs that prioritize instant clarity and familiar navigation patterns, ensuring users can navigate effortlessly without confusion.
  • Collaborate with the frontend and backend teams to ensure seamless integration of your designs.
  • Conduct usability testing to observe real users interacting with your designs, identify weaknesses, and validate design choices.
  • Develop streamlined user flows that minimize clicks and unnecessary options, enhancing decision-making and user satisfaction.
  • Maintain consistency in layouts, icons, and design elements to promote user comfort and reduce frustration.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest trends in UI/UX design and incorporate best practices into your work.
  • Focus on creating designs that not only look great but also drive user engagement and satisfaction, ultimately increasing our profits and reach.

What We’re Looking For:

  • A strong portfolio showcasing your ability to design simple, clear, and user-friendly interfaces that reduce cognitive load and enhance engagement.
  • Expertise in Figma as a design tool
  • A deep understanding of the "Don’t Make Me Think" philosophy and how to apply it to create intuitive user experiences.
  • Experience conducting user research and usability testing, with a focus on identifying and eliminating elements that force users to think or cause confusion.
  • Strong visual design skills with an eye for detail and aesthetics, ensuring designs are both beautiful and functional.
  • Ability to create familiar layouts and recognizable icons that promote seamless navigation and reduce frustration.
  • A passion for simplicity in design, with the discipline to iterate constantly and refine designs to meet user needs effectively.
  • Ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams, including developers and product managers.
